Welcome To MAJICC'21

Mohammad Ali Jinnah university International Conference on Computing 2021 (MAJICC 21) is a conference for sharing knowledge about cutting edge research and applications of computer science. The conference will bring together researchers and practitioners from academia and industry to exchange their knowledge and discuss challenges and goals for computer science and related fields.

The conference will be organized on July 15-17, 2021 by Mohammad Ali Jinnah University, Karachi. The accepted papers will be indexed by IEEE Xplore.
